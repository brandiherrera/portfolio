Portfolio

My-Brain Tracker

As my first full stack capstone project, this application uses JWT user authentication to allow users to log in so they may record a migraine, track their statistics, and view their data. The dashboard displays the users most frequent statistics in real time to they can better understand their migraines. Users may keep this information for their own knowledge or they may opt to share their findings and history with a medical professional.

Travel Destination Search

As the first of three capstone projects in my Full Stack Web Development program, this application requests to four API's to deliver curated content about the city of the user's choice. The content delivered to the user is relevant for planning travels or learning about different cities around the globe and includes information such as a brief history, trending news, travel videos, and a five day forecast.

Formula One Quiz App

As my first project submission in my Full Stack Web Development program, Formula One Quiz App is an interactive quiz app that tests the user’s knowledge of four of the most renowned Formula One racers. It calculates the user’s score and provides user feedback after each question and after completion of the quiz.

Bio
