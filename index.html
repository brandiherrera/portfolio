Formula One Quiz App

As my first project submission in my Full Stack Web Development program, Formula One Quiz App is an interactive quiz app that tests the user’s knowledge of four of the most renowned Formula One racers. It calculates the user’s score and provides user feedback after each question and after completion of the quiz.

Bio

I am a web developer and current student of Thinkful’s Full Stack Web Development program. As a middle-school student I taught myself basic HTML/CSS, but it wasn’t until recent years that I discovered how I could reignite that passion to solve problems and develop my own code for a living. I relish in taking a wireframe and bringing it to life, ensuring front and back ends work together, and learning new libraries or frameworks.

Outside of coding, you can find me lifting weights in the gym, autocrossing or driving on a race track, traveling to a new country, or fundraising my charity for peritoneal cancer research. I’m a perpetual adventuress on the hunt for my next challenge both personally and professionally.
